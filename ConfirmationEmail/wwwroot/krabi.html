FAQs about Krabi

Krabi is famous for its impressive karst scenery and breathtaking white sand beaches, where you can enjoy a variety of water sports and outdoor activities. It is also known for numerous marine parks and rainforests with abundant wildlife, mangroves, caves, and waterfalls..

The dry season (December to April) is considered the peak season for tourists as the weather is perfect for outdoors, with temperatures ranging from 23°C to 34°C. Some key events you can include on your list are the Boek Fa Andaman Festival (November), Chak Phra Festival (December), and Sea Gypsy Floating Boats Festival (around May and November).

Ao Nang and Phi Phi Islands are the most popular locations for tourists, especially for first-timers, as it offers a wide range of accommodations and is well known for its nightlife. If you are looking for an adventure, Railay and Ton Sai is the perfect place to stay to enjoy diving, hiking, kayaking, snorkeling, and trekking.
