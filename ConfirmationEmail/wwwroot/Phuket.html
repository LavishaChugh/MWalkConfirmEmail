FAQs about Phuket

Phuket is popular for its pristine beaches, but it offers so much more than its natural wonders. Discover their rich culture by exploring temples, enjoying the nightlife, watching one-of-a-kind performances, and more!

Visit anytime between November and March for the ideal season to enjoy beach activities like surfing and swimming. Phuket can be visited all year round, but you should plan accordingly when the weather gets too hot or when the monsoon season starts!

Patong offers everything from beaches, local restaurants, souvenir shops, and a busy nightlife that caters to couples, families, and friends. Phuket Old Town is also a great historical location for those who want to have a more cultural immersion.
